Our Story

SafePaw started as a small group of biologists and animal lovers. Today, we are a global community dedicated to providing stray animals with more than just survival—we provide a path back to belonging.

We believe that understanding animal behavior is the key to successful adoption. That's why our volunteers go through rigorous training in animal psychology.
